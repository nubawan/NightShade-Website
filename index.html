How It Works

Two layers. One purpose.

Single layer: 0–100%

One overlay layer takes you from 0% to 100% dim. This covers the standard range that the system brightness slider provides.

Dual layer: 100–180%

Exceed 100% and a second overlay stacks on top. The combined layers reach 180% dim, going far below the system minimum brightness without root access.

Touch pass-through

Taps pass straight through the filter to the app underneath. You never lose the ability to interact with your device, even at maximum dim.

Built-in safety

Dimming is hard-capped at 180% to keep you in control. If the screen ever goes too dark, an emergency reset instantly drops it back to 50%.
