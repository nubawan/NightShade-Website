Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2025

NightShade is a precision screen filter application. It dims and tints your screen using local system overlays. It is not a wellness app, does not track your sleep, and does not collect personal data.

1. Data Collection

NightShade collects zero personal information. We do not collect names, email addresses, device identifiers, location data, or usage analytics. No data is transmitted from your device to our servers, because we do not operate any servers.

2. Permissions Required

The application requests the following Android permissions solely to perform its core functions:

  • System Alert Window (Display over other apps): Required to draw the color filter overlay on top of the screen and across other applications.
  • Foreground Service: Required to keep the screen filter active when the app is minimized or the screen is locked. The foreground notification is mandatory for Android system stability.
  • Receive Boot Completed: Required to automatically restore your active filter preset and opacity level after the device restarts.

3. Data Storage

All user preferences—such as filter opacity, selected color, and custom presets—are stored locally on the device using Android's SharedPreferences and React Native's AsyncStorage. This data is never backed up, synced, or shared. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data.

4. Third-Party Services

NightShade does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs. There are no ads, no trackers, and no background network requests.

5. Children's Privacy

Because we collect no data whatsoever, our application is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children because we do not collect information from anyone.

6. Changes to This Policy

If the application's data practices change, this policy will be updated accordingly. All changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@nightshade.app.
