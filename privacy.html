Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2025

1. Overview

NightShade is a precision screen filter and dimming application for Android devices. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information NightShade collects, how it is used, and your choices regarding your data.

2. Data Collection

NightShade does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app functions entirely on your device. No user accounts, email addresses, device identifiers, location data, or usage analytics are collected. We do not use Firebase, Google Analytics, or any third-party analytics or crash-reporting services.

3. Permissions

NightShade requests the following Android permissions solely for its core functionality:

  • SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW — Required to display the screen filter overlay on top of other apps. This is essential for the dimming and filter feature to work.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE — Required to keep the filter active while you use other apps or when the screen is locked.

No other permissions are requested. No camera, microphone, contacts, storage, or network permissions are used.

4. Internet & Network Access

NightShade does not require internet access to function. The app operates completely offline. No data is sent to any server, cloud service, or third-party API. There are no in-app advertisements, trackers, or third-party SDKs that communicate over the network.

5. Third-Party Services

NightShade does not integrate with any third-party services, including but not limited to: social media platforms, advertising networks, analytics providers, or cloud storage services. There are no in-app purchases or payment processors.

6. Data Storage

All app preferences (dim level, selected preset, filter color) are stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in Android Settings.

7. Children's Privacy

NightShade does not knowingly collect information from children. Since we collect no data at all, our app is safe for users of all ages. No age verification is required.

8. Security

Since NightShade does not collect or transmit any data, there is minimal security risk. The overlay permission is used exclusively for screen dimming and cannot be used to capture or read screen content.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at zaytech.dev1@gmail.com.
